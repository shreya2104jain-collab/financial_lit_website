Government Policies

The Government of India implements various policies to support economic growth, healthcare, education, and infrastructure. Key policies include initiatives for financial inclusion, tax reforms, subsidies for businesses, and government schemes aimed at welfare and employment generation.

Key Government Policies

The Government of India has introduced a wide range of policies to promote development. Some of the important ones include:

  • Financial Inclusion Policies: Bringing unbanked citizens into the formal banking system (e.g., PMJDY).
  • GST Tax Reform: Simplifying indirect tax structure.
  • Make in India: Boosting domestic manufacturing and investment.
  • Digital India: Increasing access to digital services.
  • Swachh Bharat Abhiyan: Improving sanitation and cleanliness.
  • Startup India: Supporting entrepreneurship and innovation.
