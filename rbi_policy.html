RBI Policies

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) is the central bank of India and oversees the monetary policy, manages inflation, and ensures financial stability.

Key RBI Policies

Some major RBI policies include:

  • Monetary Policy: Setting interest rates, managing liquidity & inflation.
  • Financial Inclusion: PMJDY, awareness programs, banking access for all.
  • Digital Banking: Promoting UPI, NPCI, mobile payments.
  • Banking Regulation: Improving governance, capital adequacy, liquidity norms.
  • Currency Management: Issuing & maintaining secure currency notes.
