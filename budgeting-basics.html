Budgeting Basics

Learn how to create and stick to a budget to achieve your financial goals.

Why Budgeting is Important

Budgeting helps you plan how to spend your money, prioritize expenses, and achieve your financial goals.

Steps to Create a Budget

  1. Track your income and expenses.
  2. Set financial goals.
  3. Create categories for different expenses.
  4. Set limits for each category and stick to it.
