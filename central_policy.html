Central Bank Policies

The Central Bank regulates monetary policy, inflation and ensures financial stability in the country.

Key Policies of the Central Bank

  • Monetary Policy: Controls inflation and interest rates.
  • Financial Stability: Prevents financial crises.
  • Currency Management: Ensures supply of legal tender.
  • Bank Supervision: Regulates commercial banks.
  • Exchange Rate Policy: Maintains stable forex market.
  • Developmental Role: Promotes financial inclusion.
