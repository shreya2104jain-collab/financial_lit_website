Key Policies of the Central Bank

The Central Bank implements several key policies:

  • Monetary Policy: Controls inflation using repo & reverse repo rates.
  • Financial Stability: Ensures safe banking systems.
  • Currency Management: Issues and manages money supply.
  • Bank Supervision: Regulates commercial banks.
  • Exchange Rate Policy: Maintains forex market stability.
  • Developmental Role: Supports financial inclusion in rural areas.
